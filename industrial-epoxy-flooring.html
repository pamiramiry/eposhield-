Industries We Serve

Industrial Environments We Coat

EpoShield has installed industrial coating systems for a wide range of sectors across Southern Ontario, each with unique performance requirements.

Manufacturing FacilitiesCoatings engineered to handle heavy machinery loads, chemical spills, abrasion, and constant foot and forklift traffic. Safety line marking available.

Food & Beverage ProcessingCementitious urethane and quartz systems providing hygienic, non-porous surfaces that withstand thermal shock from cleaning and food processing operations.

Warehouses & Distribution CentresHigh-build solid epoxy with excellent abrasion resistance for areas subject to constant pallet jack, forklift, and heavy goods traffic.

Pharmaceutical & Chemical PlantsChemical-resistant systems and ESD coatings for environments requiring strict containment and electrostatic control.

Aerospace & AutomotiveCoatings that resist oil, hydraulic fluid, fuel, and heavy structural loads — for aircraft hangars, service bays, and manufacturing floors.

Power GenerationRobust systems offering chemical resistance, thermal stability, and protection against oils and lubricants in demanding energy facility environments.
