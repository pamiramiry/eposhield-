About This Service

A Better Floor for Your Home

Epoxy coatings have long been the contractor's choice for residential concrete surfaces. They deliver a hard, seamless, chemical-resistant finish that's far more durable than paint — and dramatically more attractive than bare concrete.

EpoShield installs residential epoxy systems across Southern Ontario for garages, basements, utility rooms, and outdoor spaces. Every installation includes thorough surface preparation, industrial-grade materials, and our 5-year warranty.
