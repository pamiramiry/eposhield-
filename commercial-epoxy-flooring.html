Commercial Environments We Coat

The Case for Epoxy

Why Commercial Properties Choose Epoxy

01

Long-Term Cost Savings

Properly installed epoxy lasts years longer than alternative flooring — reducing replacement cycles, maintenance costs, and downtime for your business.

02

Seamless & Hygienic

No grout lines or seams mean no place for bacteria, debris, or moisture to accumulate — critical for food service and healthcare adjacent environments.

03

Rapid Installation

Most commercial installations are completed with minimal disruption to business operations. Polyaspartic systems can be ready for light traffic within hours.

04

Custom Aesthetics

Choose from a range of colours, finishes, and textures to complement your brand and interior design — from high-gloss retail floors to matte industrial finishes.
