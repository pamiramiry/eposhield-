About Us

Concrete Coating
Contractors Who Care.

EpoShield is a specialist concrete floor coating company serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Southern Ontario. We're committed to installing the best, safest products possible — and standing behind our work with a 5-year warranty.

Our Mission

Focused on Your Investment

At EpoShield, we're committed to installing the best, safest products possible to make sure your investment lasts for many years with outstanding performance and durability.

We believe that exceptional concrete coating work starts long before the first material hits the floor. It begins with honest consultation, careful surface assessment, and choosing the right system for your specific conditions — not the fastest one to apply.

Our crews bring the technical knowledge and practical experience to navigate the full spectrum of concrete conditions, from fresh residential slabs to decades-old industrial floors with contamination and damage. Whatever your floor looks like today, we know how to make it better.

Our Values

How We Work

Craft

The Work Speaks for Itself

We don't cut corners on preparation, materials, or application. Proper surface prep takes time. The right system costs more upfront. We do it anyway — because a floor that fails in two years is a failure for everyone.

Integrity

Straightforward Advice

We'll tell you what system is actually right for your space, not what upsells best. If your floor doesn't need a premium decorative system, we'll say so. Our reputation is built on referrals — honesty is the business model.

Expertise

Technical Knowledge Applied

Concrete is not a consistent material. Moisture, contamination, damage, and previous coatings all affect what system will work and how it must be applied. Our crew knows how to read a slab and respond to what they find.

Partnership

Support Beyond Installation

We don't disappear after the final topcoat. Questions about maintenance, warranty claims, or additional areas — we're here. Your project doesn't end when we pack up the equipment.
