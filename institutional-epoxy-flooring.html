Who We Serve

Institutional Environments We Coat

Institutional facilities share a common set of requirements: surfaces that withstand high daily use, meet regulatory and infection-control standards, and minimize lifetime maintenance costs. EpoShield's systems are used across all of them.

Schools & UniversitiesHigh-traffic corridors, gymnasiums, cafeterias, labs, and locker rooms — seamless, graffiti-resistant coatings that hold up to heavy student use and daily custodial cleaning.

Hospitals & Medical FacilitiesInfection-control compliant, seamless, non-porous surfaces for operating rooms, patient corridors, labs, and utility rooms. ESD-safe systems available for medical electronics environments.

Government & Civic BuildingsLobbies, offices, washrooms, and service corridors in courthouses, municipal buildings, and federal facilities — professional finishes that reduce maintenance costs over decades.

Arenas & Recreation CentresDurable, slip-resistant coatings for arenas, pool surrounds, weight rooms, and change facilities. Polyurethane systems handle freeze-thaw conditions at rink-adjacent areas.

Places of WorshipDecorative and durable floor coatings for sanctuaries, halls, kitchens, and service areas — available in a range of finishes to complement the character of the space.

Libraries & Cultural FacilitiesLow-VOC, low-odour systems suited for occupied spaces — quiet to install, quick to cure, and finished to the high aesthetic standard these institutions require.

Meeting the Standard

What Institutional Projects Require

01

Code Compliance

Institutional floors must meet building codes for slip resistance, flame spread ratings, and in some cases chemical resistance. Our systems are specified to meet these standards — we can provide documentation for tenders and inspection.

02

Infection Control

Seamless, non-porous surfaces provide no harbour for bacteria or mould. For healthcare environments, we install systems specifically designed to meet infection control protocols — impervious to the harsh disinfectants used in clinical settings.

03

Minimal Downtime

Institutional facilities often cannot close for extended periods. We work within your operational constraints — scheduling around holidays, nights, and weekends when needed — and use fast-cure polyaspartic systems where turnaround time is critical.

04

Long Service Life

Institutional budgets favour lifetime value over initial cost. A well-installed epoxy system in a school corridor will outlast vinyl tile, polished concrete, and ceramic alternatives — with lower maintenance costs across its lifespan.

Our Approach

How We Work on Institutional Projects

01

Site Assessment & Specification

We review your facility's requirements, usage patterns, regulatory standards, and any tender documentation before recommending a system. We can provide written specifications for architect and owner review.

02

Surface Preparation

Diamond grinding and shot blasting to the concrete surface profile required for your chosen system. Moisture testing and concrete repair where required. Proper preparation is the single most critical factor in long-term performance.

03

System Installation

Application by our experienced crew using the materials specified. For healthcare and food service environments, low-VOC, low-odour formulations are available — allowing work to proceed in or near occupied areas.

04

Handover & Documentation

Final walk-through, cure and maintenance instructions, and written warranty documentation. For tendered projects, we can provide product data sheets, safety data sheets, and installation records.
