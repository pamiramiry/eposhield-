Coating Systems

Complete System Catalogue

01

Metallic Epoxy Coatings

A seamless blend of epoxy resins with nano-sized metallic pigments, producing a resilient floor with a captivating iridescent and pearlescent finish. No two installations look identical — each is a unique surface.

Residential Decorative Commercial

02

Decorative Flake Coatings

Epoxy resin combined with decorative vinyl flakes delivers a functional, visually appealing surface. Available in dozens of colour blends. Highly durable, slip-resistant, and easy to maintain.

Residential Garage Commercial

03

Decorative Quartz Coatings

Coloured quartz aggregate combined with epoxy resin, chemical-resistant grout, and seal coats. Produces a dense, non-porous surface with superior texture and slip resistance — ideal for wet environments.

Institutional Commercial Wet Areas

04

Moisture Vapor Barrier Coatings

Engineered to block moisture vapor transmission through concrete slabs — protecting the coating system and substrate from moisture damage, mold growth, and coating delamination.

Residential Basement Industrial

05

Traffic Toppings

High-performance membranes designed for vehicular and pedestrian traffic exposure. Provides exceptional protection against moisture infiltration, deicing salts, alkalis, and acids — the standard choice for parking structures and exposed decks.

Parking Structures Exposed Decks Commercial

06

Polyurethane Waterproofing Membranes

A robust shield against water infiltration with superior elongation and crack-bridging properties. Protects concrete from weathering, UV exposure, and structural movement while maintaining a seamless finish.

Waterproofing Rooftop Decks Balconies

07

Electrostatic Dissipating (ESD) Coatings

Conductive and anti-static flooring systems that safely dissipate electrostatic charge — protecting sensitive electronics, equipment, and personnel in precision manufacturing and technology environments.

Manufacturing Electronics Industrial

08

Solid Epoxy Coatings

Single-colour epoxy systems providing excellent chemical resistance and a clean, professional appearance. Available with an optional polyurethane topcoat for enhanced abrasion resistance and UV stability.

Industrial Commercial Chemical Resistance

09

Cementitious Urethane

A polyurethane resin screed system combining the mechanical strength of cement with the chemical resistance of urethane. The premier choice for food and beverage processing, commercial kitchens, and heavy industrial environments requiring thermal shock resistance.

Food & Beverage Industrial Thermal Resistance
How It Works

The Installation Process

01

Site Assessment

We evaluate your concrete substrate — moisture levels, existing damage, surface profile, and use requirements — before recommending a system.

02

Surface Preparation

Proper prep is everything. We grind, shot-blast, or diamond-grind the surface to the profile required for a permanent mechanical bond.

03

System Application

Each layer is applied to specification — primer, base coat, broadcast layer (if applicable), and topcoat — using professional equipment and techniques.

04

Final Inspection

We walk through the finished installation with you, explain care and maintenance, and confirm the 5-year warranty before we leave the site.
